MVP implementation checklist

  1. 01 Auth0 roles, gateway routing, and seller onboarding.
  2. 02 Catalog upload flow with metadata and S3/MinIO quarantine storage.
  3. 03 Validation worker for SCL, DB, UDT, and ZIP packages.
  4. 04 Admin moderation queue with audit logs and seller trust tiers.
  5. 05 Stripe Connect checkout, platform fees, and entitlement creation.
  6. 06 Signed CloudFront downloads, revocation, and download audit trail.
  7. 07 Elasticsearch indexing, transactional email, and health alerts.
